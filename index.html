ABOUT

Hello! My name is Zach Waterson. I'm a proud member of the Notre Dame Class of 2016, studying Computer Science with a minor in Philosophy, Politics, and Economics. I am originally from the San Francisco Bay Area, but I chose to attend college in the Midwest so as to experience this "winter" thing that I've heard about.

I am extremely passionate about technology, especially the ways in which it enriches the day-to-day lives of others. Growing up just outside the Silicon Valley has infected me with an entrepreneurial spirit, which I brought with me to Notre Dame. I love witnessing a family member be impressed with a feature on his or her phone, and I'm the first to show a friend a new app that I think can enrich his or her life.
